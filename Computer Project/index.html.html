A Comprehensive Analysis of Government Politics, Accountability, and the Recent Senate Investigation into Flood Control Corruption

Profile Overview

SD

Cezarah Rowena "Sarah" Cruz Discaya

Filipino Businesswoman & Politician | Major Government Contractor

Background: Sarah Discaya is associated with construction firms "Alpha & Omega Construction" and "St. Gerrard Construction Corporation," which were labeled among the top contractors for various flood control projects during the Rodrigo Duterte and Bongbong Marcos administrations.

Known For: Being a businesswoman and major government contractor whose companies have handled hundreds of flood control and infrastructure projects across the Philippines.

Controversial Issues & Senate Hearings

September 1, 2025 Senate Hearing

The controversy culminated in a major Senate hearing where Sarah Discaya was grilled about alleged ghost projects and her family's nine companies.

Ghost Projects Allegations

Sarah Discaya faced intense questioning about alleged ghost projects - flood control infrastructure that was supposedly built but may not exist or function properly. She denied involvement in any ghost projects during Senate testimony.

Bid Rigging Concerns

Discaya admitted that her nine construction firms simultaneously participated in bidding for the same government flood control projects, raising questions about fair competition and potential bid manipulation.

Kickback Allegations

During Senate hearings, Discaya accused several lawmakers and engineers of receiving kickbacks in exchange for public infrastructure contracts, claiming cooperation with public officials was necessary to gain projects.

Political Connections

The couple named at least 17 House representatives and other officials in their explosive testimony, claiming they often name-dropped Speaker Martin Romualdez and other prominent politicians.

Company Delisting

Following the controversy, Discaya firms were delisted from PhilGEPS (Philippine Government Electronic Procurement System), effectively barring them from future government contracts.

Contempt Charges

Curlee Discaya, Sarah's husband, was held in contempt for allegedly lying about Sarah's health during a Senate hearing when she failed to appear, claiming she was ill.

Background Check & Analysis

Business Empire Overview

Sarah and Curlee Discaya built a construction empire through multiple companies that dominated Philippines flood control projects. Their companies were among 15 firms that secured US$1.75 billion worth of government flood control projects, earning them the informal title of "King and Queen of Flood Control."

Key Companies:

  • Alpha & Omega Construction - Primary construction firm
  • St. Gerrard Construction Corporation - Major flood control contractor
  • Seven other family firms - Various construction companies

Political Involvement:

Beyond business, Sarah Discaya entered politics, allegedly being "provoked" by incumbent Pasig mayor Vico Sotto to run for mayor. She claimed her family faced defamation since Sotto became mayor in 2019 and that he hindered her business operations.

Current Status

As of September 2025, the Discaya case represents one of the most significant corruption investigations in recent Philippine political history, highlighting issues of government accountability and transparency in public infrastructure projects.
